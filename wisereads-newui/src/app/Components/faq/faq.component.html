Frequently asked Questions

How do I create an account on the wisereads portal?

Once you entered the wisreads portal. Click on the register at the right hand corner of the home page. Fill in the required details and click on register button. Your account has been created.

How can I login to my account?

Please click on "Login" at the top right hand corner of the home page. Now enter your registered email ID and Password and press login button.

How to borrow books online:

1. Sign in if you are an existing user or register as new user.  2. Select book you want to borrow by clicking on book in the home page (also search for a book). Click on checkout. Select checkout date, due date and pay advance amount and then click on checkout. Your checkout order is placed.

How to return a book:

1. Login to your account. 2. Go to my books 3. Go to delivered books and click on check-in. Your Return order has been placed.

How long can I keep a book with me once I have picked it up?

We do not have a cap on the number of days that you can keep a book with you. You can keep a book for as long as you want. However we request you to return the book in a reasonable amount of time since other members may also be in the waiting list.

What is the process of borrowing of a book?

Select your books of your choice. Click on checkout. We’ll delivery them for free at your doorstep. Click on checkin We’ll pick it up once you are done by paying the rent amount.
